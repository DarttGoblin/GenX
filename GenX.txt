Project: GenX

Title: GenX: AI-Powered Poster Generation

Description:
GenX is a deep learning project that fine-tunes a pretrained model to generate movie, series, 
or show posters based on a given title and story description. The project leverages text-to-image 
generation techniques to create visually compelling and thematically aligned posters that capture 
the essence of a given narrative. By training on a dataset of existing posters labeled with their 
corresponding titles and stories, GenX learns to understand artistic composition, typography, and 
genre-specific aesthetics. The model will generate posters in a diverse range of styles while 
maintaining coherence with the input text. This project can be useful for filmmakers, content 
creators, and media platforms looking for AI-assisted poster design. The approach involves optimizing 
a diffusion-based or transformer-based model to enhance text-to-image alignment, ensuring that the 
generated posters effectively represent the mood, themes, and key visual elements of the given story. 
Future extensions may include style customization and real-time poster refinement.
